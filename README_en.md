# NS Engine  
A simple external thinking system using AI

---

## What this is

People often try to think only inside their mind.

This can lead to:

- confusion  
- fixed ideas  
- overthinking  
- emotional bias  

NS Engine is a simple method to change this.

It uses AI as an external thinking layer.

---

## Core idea

Do not keep thinking only inside your head.

Put your thoughts outside.

Use AI to structure them.

Then rebuild your thinking with clarity.

---

## How to use

1. Open an AI tool  
2. Write your thought  
3. Paste it into the AI  
4. Ask it to analyze it using structured steps  

---

## Thinking process (NS Engine flow)

Write → Break → Question → Reverse → Rebuild

---

## Trigger instructions for AI

Ask the AI to:

### 1. Break the idea
Separate facts and interpretations.

### 2. Find assumptions
Identify hidden beliefs.

### 3. Turn assumptions into conditions
Clarify when something is true or not true.

### 4. Generate opposite views
Show alternative perspectives.

### 5. Rebuild the idea
Return a simple, structured version.

---

## Example

Input:

“I think young people use smartphones too much.”

The AI response process will:

- separate fact and opinion  
- expose assumptions  
- show alternative perspectives  
- reduce fixed judgment  
- rebuild a clearer view
