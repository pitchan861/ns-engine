# NS Engine — Triggers (English)

## Core idea

Use AI as an external thinking system.

Do not try to solve everything in your head.

Write your thought and give it to AI.

---

## 1. Break the idea

Ask AI to decompose your thought.

- What are the parts?
- What is fact vs interpretation?

---

## 2. Find assumptions

Ask AI to identify hidden assumptions.

- What am I assuming without proof?
- What might not be true?

---

## 3. Turn assumptions into conditions

Convert vague beliefs into conditions.

- When is this true?
- When is it not true?

---

## 4. Generate opposite views

Ask AI to show alternative perspectives.

- What is the opposite view?
- How would someone disagree?

---

## 5. Rebuild the idea

Ask AI to reconstruct your thought.

- Simple
- Clear
- Structured

---

## Basic rule

Do not try to think correctly first.

Just write your thought.

Let AI structure it.

---

## Minimal workflow

Write → Break → Question → Reverse → Rebuild

---

## Core principle

You are not replacing thinking.

You are extending it.

AI is your external cognitive layer.
